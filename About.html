Freetime

I've been playing Handball pretty much since i can remember, i think i started when i was around 7 years old. Handball is one of the biggest sports in Denmark and i miss it so much here, since it's not really a thing in the US. I come from a pretty small town with a population of around 1000 people, so it was always tough to find enough people for a team in my age group, so from when i was around 15 years old i just played in the adult leagues, which was pretty hard in the beginning, but today i definitely think i've become a much better player because of it.
